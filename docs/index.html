Market Station Apartments – Conveniently Located and Affordable

At Market Station Apartments you will find a perfect blend of convenience, affordability and comfort. If you ask our residents what they love about living here, they will tell you that Market Station offers great value: classic floor plans with plenty of storage at a terrific price. They will say that Market Station gives them easy access to shopping and is only minutes from I-40 and I-73 making it a short commute to work anywhere in the Triad area. Many of our residents appreciate living on the Greenboro Transit Bus Line with direct service to downtown. They also love the beautiful landscaping and elegant trees which provide lots of shade. So whether you’re looking for a studio, one-bedroom or two-bedroom apartment, stop by and check out everything Market Station has to offer. You’ll be glad you did.
